About IncubeES

Empowering Startups, Transforming Ideas into Reality!

IncubES 2025 is an initiative by Techno International New Town (TINT), a premier institution under the Techno India Group, one of Eastern India’s largest education networks fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and industry-academia collaboration.

A Legacy of Excellence in Education & Innovation

• 40+ years of academic leadership in engineering, management, and technology.
• A vast network of universities, colleges, and research centres driving impactful change.
• Strong industry tie-ups for entrepreneurship and skill development.

A Hub of Innovation & Emerging Talent

• A leading institution focused on technology, business, and startup incubation.
• State-of-the-art infrastructure fostering research, entrepreneurship, and leadership.
• A dedicated Business Incubation Cell nurturing young entrepreneurs.
