Investors & VCs

Investors, especially Venture Capitalists (VCs), play a crucial role in funding startups and early-stage businesses, providing capital and expertise in exchange for equity, while angel investors often focus on seed-stage funding.

Startup Mentors

An experienced individual who provides guidance, advice, and support to entrepreneurs and early-stage startups, helping them navigate challenges and make informed decisions.

Business Leaders

A leader is someone who guides and influences others to achieve a common goal, setting direction, making decisions, and managing resources to drive success and foster a positive work environment.

Industry Experts

Industry experts are individuals with extensive knowledge, experience, and a proven track record in a specific field, recognized for their expertise and thought leadership .
